Do you like dogs? Do you specially like Corgis? This is the club for you!
Our small little club is dedicated to them: Pembroke, Cardigan, Mixed, you name it!
Even if your dog is not a Corgi or don't own a dog you are welcome!

Activities

These are our regular activities:

  • Meet and greet
  • Dogs playtime
  • Training session
  • Dogs and kids playtime
  • Pics and treats

There are special events such as costume contest, racing, treasure hunt and more!

Join us!

Our meeting place is the Clearing in the Woods Park, Woods Av.
Every Sunday at 10 am near the main fountain.

About Us

We are a small group of people who like Corgis and dogs of all sizes.
Our main interest is building a place for our small friends to play and make friends.
